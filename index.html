WELCOME TO THE SPCA OF NORTHERN NEVADA

Thank you for visiting the website of the SPCA of Northern Nevada. We were established by volunteers in 1998 as Nevada’s first no-kill animal shelter. Our mission is to be an innovative regional leader in responsible treatment of homeless dogs and cats, primarily through their rescue and placement in forever loving homes, and by promoting spaying and neutering to control pet overpopulation. We accomplish our mission with dedication to our core values and a lifetime commitment to our animals.
